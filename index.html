OSS Pledge

Launching in October

Open Source is a restaurant.

  • not a grocery store (pay first, eat after)
  • not a soup kitchen (someone else pays)
  • but a restaurant (eat first, pay after)

Our companies feast year after year at the Open Source table. It's time to settle up.

How

Step 1: Pay Open Source maintainers.

The minimum to participate is $2,000 per full-time employed developer per year.

Step 2: Self-report annually:
  1. number of full-time employed developers
  2. payments to maintainers by platform or foundation
